About our Restaurant

History

Tasty Restaurant began on April 26th, 2009 in the city of Johannesburg. Focusing on the goals of “Quality, Healthy, and Tradition” the company is family operation making each dish in our own kitchens from scratch. Tasty Restaurant has three generations of family member working together to fulfill the idea “Honor the Customer, Quality Comes First”. Through hard work and dedication, Tasty Restaurant began building its brand around the idea of delicious and authentic traditional south african food.


Our goal

We aim to take simple ingredients in its most basic form and transform it into delicious products for our customers. Our restaurants make every item from scratch; this means we make every item from its most original form whether it be flour, salt, oil, and sesame for our famous oven baked flaky buns or freshly ground pork, cabbage, rice noodles, and flour for our vegetable pork buns.
